Symbolic Differentiation

This is a symbolic differentiation and simplification system written in Emacs Lisp. In accordance with Lisp's code-as-data philosophy, it operates on Lisp's S-expressions. It makes good use of macros to make extension easier. After a few more functions are added, the differentiation module should work on any Elementary Function. Unfortunately, the output of the differentiation module is often very complex and the simplification module does not work well on many inputs. I worked out how to do these two tasks by thinking about how I do them myself. I did not consult the existing literature on computational algebra in doing this, though I may do so someday for comparison's sake.

Source

ddx.el simplify.el

Example

ELISP> (dd 'x '(sin (* 2 x)))
(*
 (+
  (* 2 1)
  (* 0 x))
 (cos
  (* 2 x)))

ELISP> (simp (dd 'x '(sin (* 2 x))))
(* 2
   (cos
    (* 2 x)))
