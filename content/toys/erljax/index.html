Erlang/Ajax Toy

This is a simple web "game" made using yaws and jQuery. I wanted to try using Erlang to create a real-time, multiplayer game. I decided the best way to handle both the networking and GUI was with web technologies. What I ended up with is a proof-of-concept consisting of a grid of X's and O's, that may be toggled by clicking on a cell. The interesting part is that the grid is persistent and synced between all users viewing the page. A few years later, just for fun, I worked with a sister to add sounds when cells are clicked on.
